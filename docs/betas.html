4 Betas

Definition 4.1 Given two instruments \(X, Y\) the following definitions of \(\beta_{X\to Y}\) are equivalent:

  • The variance of the portfolio \(Y-\beta X\) is minimized.
  • If I know that the return of \(X\) today is \(p\), then my best linear guess to the return of \(Y\) is \(\beta_{X\to Y} p\).
